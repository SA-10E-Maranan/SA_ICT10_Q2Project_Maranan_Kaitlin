About Our Institute

The Starveil Institute of Magic and Arts is a distinguished institution devoted to nurturing gifted casters and guiding them in the mastery of magical arts. With a rich tradition of enchanting education spanning generations, we take pride in fostering both skill and wisdom in every student.

Our mystical halls, expert faculty, and immersive curriculum provide an environment where students can excel academically, creatively, and socially, preparing them to wield magic responsibly and explore the wonders of the magical world.

Our Paths

Path of Arts and Aeriality

Casters explore the intersection magic, art, and movement. From enchanted music to broom-riding, they learn to harness magic as a medium of creativity. Training emphasizes imagination, coordination, and showmanship, encouraging Casters to stay creative.

Path of Enchantment and Crafting

This path centers on the creation and enhancement of magical objects. Casters learn how to imbue everyday items with spells, craft charms and talismans, and design enchanted tools for practical or artistic use.

Path of Conjuration & Summoning

Casters learn to safely summon, control, and bind magical entities, from tiny familiars to complex constructs. This path emphasizes precision, responsibility, and creative application of conjuration magic.

Path of Elemental & Environmental Manipulation

This path trains casters to harness elemental energies for combat, protection, or artistic expression. Casters also explore environmental magic, such as enhancing flora or influencing weather patterns.

Path of Mind & Reality Studies

Reserved for the most talented Casters, this path explores telepathy, illusions, spatial manipulation, and interdimensional studies. Ethical responsibility, control, and deep theoretical knowledge are essential components of this path.
